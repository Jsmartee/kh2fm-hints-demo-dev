This hint system has two categories of items: "Keys to Light" and "Report Items". The "Keys to Light" pool is the same as the important check pool from the original hint system. The "Report Items" pool has useful abilities..

Keys to Light:

  • 3 Proofs
  • Promise Charm
  • 5 Drive Forms (Final Form can be excluded)
  • 4 Summons
  • Magic (18 total, Cure can be excluded)
  • 5 Torn Pages (can be excluded)
  • 13 Ansem Reports (can be excluded)

Report Items:

  • Second Chance
  • Once More
  • Guard
  • Horizontal Slash
  • Finishing Leap
  • Slide Dash
  • Guard Break
  • Explosion
  • Magnet Burst
  • Trinity
  • Scan
  • Aerial Recovery
  • Combo Master
  • Finishing Plus
  • Negative Combo
  • Berserk Charge
  • Experience Boost
  • Light and Darkness

Info:

  • Each hint is structured as "[World] has [number] Keys to Light and [number] Report Items."
  • The same logic as the other system applies to this one too. (Proofs must be hinted, Proof on forms mean all forms must be hinted, etc.)
  • The Report Items will not be hinted if they're on keyblades.
