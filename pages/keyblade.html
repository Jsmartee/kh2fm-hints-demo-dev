Guiding Keys

This version of the hint system has keyblades and other key items hint to an associated world for items required to beat the seed and the 13 reports hints towards optional items.

General Format

Each world has an associated keyblade or key item that will reveal a hint. For example, picking up Hero's Crest will reveal a hint saying "Olympus Coliseum has 2 Keys to Light." This means that world has 2 items from the Keys to Light list. The Keys to Light list contains items only required to beat the seed, so unlike the important checks system, summons will not be hinted. Instead, summons will be put in the Report Items list and have a chance of being hinted by the 13 reports. In this version of the hint system, every world is hinted since all of these items can be picked up in a seed.

The Report Items list contains things that are not required to beat the seed, but may be useful. This can include summons, abilities, and optional magic like Cure. 13 of the items from the list are randomly chosen in a seed and hinted by a report. Examples of this hint can be "Reports show that Chicken Little is in Hollow Bastion." or "Reports show that Explosion is in Disney Castle."
